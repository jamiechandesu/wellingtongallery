Ingrid Boot was born in the UK and emigrated to New Zealand in 2000. She studied art at London's Westminster University followed by a degree in Illustration from De Montfort University.
Her paintings predominantly portray the beauty of the female form. There is an essence of timeless elegance which is encapsulated within all of Ingrid's work. Her inspiration comes from a love of fashion history and movie icons of a bygone era.
